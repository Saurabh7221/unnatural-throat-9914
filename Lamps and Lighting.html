Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


What Should Be The Ideal Wattage Of The Floor Lamp?


Most floor lamps should range between 250-350 watts and can be supplied with two or three bulbs.


How Do You Decorate Your Home With Smart Lights?


When you buy smart lights for your home, think of which area you want to install them. Start from the interior and then move to the exterior.


What Is The Right Light Bulb Colour For The Night If You Use A Chandelier?


You can go for the fluorescent or red light as these two colours dont contradict the circadian rhythm. A blue or green should be avoided, as waking up to these colours may cause a strain on the eye.


What Is The Ideal Height Of A Table Lamp?


If your table lamp is in your living room or drawing room, make sure it meets eye level and should be within 24 to 34 inches. If you want to watch TV or just read a book without spreading the light to other surrounding areas, there would be plenty of light.
